About Me

I love creating and managing complex systems. I was born in Italy in 2003, and since a very young age, I have been in love with technology. I find happiness in every problem I manage to solve, every solution I optimize, and every person I help. Every day, I strive to learn as much as possible to improve both myself and the software I provide.

I have a formal education with strong foundations in mathematics, physics, chemistry, biology, and, more broadly, science. I specialize in software engineering, which I have been practicing for well over a decade. My first experiences in programming began by developing mods for video games. Since then, I have worked on countless projects, such as standalone applications, web applications, embedded software, APIs, frameworks, and more.

If you'd like to learn more about me, feel free to contact me, and we'll schedule a meeting.
