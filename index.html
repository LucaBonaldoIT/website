About Me

Passionate about technology and problem-solving, I was born in Italy in 2003 and have been immersed in the world of tech since childhood. I thrive on tackling challenges and crafting innovative solutions that drive efficiency and success.

With a strong foundation in science and formal education in mathematics, physics, chemistry, and biology, I ventured into software engineering at a young age. My journey began with developing mods for video games and evolved into a career marked by projects ranging from standalone applications to complex web systems, embedded software, and APIs.

My work is fueled by a desire to continuously learn and improve, both personally and professionally. I am committed to delivering high-quality software solutions that not only meet but exceed expectations.
